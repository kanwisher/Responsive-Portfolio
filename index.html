About Me

David Kanwisher is an up and coming web developer in the Charlotte area. David has experience in HTML5, CSS3, and Bootstrap. Previously, David worked as the Service Manager for the 3rd largest pest control company in the country. Lightning struck and a new passion was discovered: Creating dynamic and beautiful websites and applications. He graduated with honors from the University of North Carolina at Charlotte with a degree in Business Administration with a concentraton in Marketing. David spends his free time coding, learning new web technologies and spending time with his family. David is currently working on projects, but is open to new work and opportunities.
